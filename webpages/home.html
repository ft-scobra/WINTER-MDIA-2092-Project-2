Welcome to Dial-Back, where everything has its time!

Dial-Back offers unique items from different eras, but there's a catch: each item is available for only one day! With Dial-Back, we offer a variety of items based on the year the app takes for that day. For example, if the year is 1975, some items up for sale would be mood rings, rotary phones, and a range of clothing, as they were the items trending at that point in time. One day could be 2008, while the next could be 1960, so make sure to clock in and grab your favourite historic items before the 24-hour timeline resets!
